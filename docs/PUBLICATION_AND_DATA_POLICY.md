# Publication And Data Policy

Journal issue 137 is a public scientific publication once approved for release.

Public by default after approval:

- final DOCX/PDF;
- published article text;
- author names as printed in the journal;
- article titles;
- published DOI, UDC, and ORCID values;
- page renders, contact sheets, and release audits.

Not public by default:

- applications and submission forms;
- internal Excel registers;
- private email addresses and phone numbers;
- payment data;
- unpublished drafts;
- absolute local paths;
- tokens and secrets;
- model weights.

Public reports must use sanitized counts, internal IDs, relative paths, and
non-sensitive technical findings. Private source material remains in the private
workspace unless explicitly approved for publication.
